Biography

I am Biljana Cvetkoska, born 08.12.1987 in Skopje, Macedonia. In 2010/2011 I was postgraduate studies at Sts ''Cyril and Mathodius'' University-Faculty of Art-Skopje, the department of painting; also an elective subject Graphic and Grapchical tehnology. In 2006/2007-2009/2010 I past Sts ''Cyril and Methodius'' University-Faculty of Art, graduated at the department of painting, a couse in conservation and restoration of easel and wall painting. In 2002/2003-2005/2006 I finisht High school ''Lazar Licenoski'' Course in painting. In 2001/2002, I attenden the course in drawing and painting.
